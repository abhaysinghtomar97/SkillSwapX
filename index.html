How SkillSwapX Works

1. Upload Your Skill Course

Record a short course on something you're good at. It could be anything — design, coding, music, etc.

2. Earn SkillCoins

Every time someone watches your content, you earn SkillCoins. No need to repeat it live every time!

3. Spend SkillCoins to Learn

Use your SkillCoins to watch other courses or book 1-on-1 weekend sessions with top mentors.
